# GuardianLink: Next-Gen Crisis Coordination for Hospitality

## 🛡️ The Mission
In a crisis, every second counts. **GuardianLink** is a decentralized emergency response ecosystem designed specifically for the hospitality industry. It bridges the critical communication gap between distressed guests, hotel security, and first responders during high-stakes events like fires, floods, or medical emergencies.

---

## 🛑 The Problem
When an emergency hits a hotel:
1. **The "App Barrier":** Guests never download hotel-specific apps for short stays. In a crisis, they have no digital tools to help them.
2. **Fragmented Info:** Security teams are overwhelmed by hundreds of calls, making it impossible to see the "big picture."
3. **Language Barriers:** International guests often cannot communicate effectively with local emergency services.
4. **Indoor Blindness:** Standard GPS stops working at the front door, leaving responders blind to where people are trapped inside.

---

## 💡 The Idea: "Zero-Install Safety"
AegisNode replaces chaos with a **Three-Tier Mesh System**:
- **For Guests:** A Zero-Install **Progressive Web App (PWA)** accessed via QR codes or hotel Wi-Fi. No download needed.
- **For Staff:** A high-performance **Command Center** to manage the crisis in real-time.
- **For Responders:** A **Tactical Bridge** providing live indoor heatmaps and floor-by-floor data.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 1. Dynamic "Safe-Path" Navigation
Unlike static exit signs, AegisNode uses Leaflet with OpenStreetMap and indoor overlays to draw a live path on the guest's phone. If sensors detect danger in a specific hallway, the AI automatically reroutes guests to a safer, alternative exit.

### 2. Gemini AI "Chaos Filter"
Our backend uses **Gemini 3 Flash** to analyze hundreds of incoming reports (text, audio, and video). It merges duplicate alerts into one "Incident Entity" and provides staff with a 3-sentence summary of what is actually happening.

### 3. Real-time Multilingual SOS
A trapped guest can speak into the app in their native language. Our AI-powered bridge translates their distress call for the local staff and first responders instantly, ensuring help arrives regardless of language barriers.

### 4. Digital Triage Heatmap
AegisNode tracks the "safe/unsafe" status of all active devices. Hotel security can see a live heatmap of the building:
- **Green:** Guests who have reached the assembly point.
- **Red:** Stationary devices in rooms where an SOS was triggered.

---

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack (2026 Modern Web)
- **Frontend:** Next.js 16 (App Router) + Tailwind CSS v4.
- **Mobile Layer:** Capacitor (for native staff alerts) and PWA (for zero-install guest access).
- **Intelligence:** Google Gemini 3 Flash (Multimodal analysis & translation).
- **Real-time Engine:** Firebase (Firestore & Cloud Messaging) for <100ms sync.
- **Location:** OpenStreetMap + Leaflet (custom markers, overlays, and indoor routing).
